Our Mission

At BabyPDF, we believe that PDF tools should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've created a suite of free PDF tools that work entirely in your browser, with no uploads, no registration, and no hidden costs.

Why Choose BabyPDF?

100% Secure

Your files never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Fast Processing

Process PDF files quickly without waiting for server uploads and downloads.

Completely Free

No subscriptions, no limits, no hidden charges. All tools are free forever.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, BabyPDF started as a simple project to help people work with PDF files more efficiently. We noticed that many PDF tools required expensive subscriptions or uploaded files to servers, compromising privacy.

We built BabyPDF to solve these problems. Our tools work 100% in your browser using modern web technologies, ensuring your documents stay private and secure.
